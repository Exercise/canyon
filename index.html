How It works

A Personalized Approach

At Canyon Ranch we know that weight loss is not a one size fits all process, so your program will be personalized to meet your individual needs. Your team of coaches will help you identify goals and design the best strategy—for you—to achieve success.

Your Team of Expert Coaches

You will get customized advice and guidance from a team of coaches in 3 different areas—nutritionists to create a healthy eating plan you can stick with, exercise physiologists to help you find activities you enjoy, and a dedicated life coach to support you throughout your weight loss journey.

Convenient Video Coaching

We will work with you to book your video coaching sessions at times that work for your busy schedule. And the best part—you can conduct a session from home, work or anywhere you can connect to the Internet.

Daily Advice from Canyon Ranch

For over 35 years, we have been helping people lose weight and live a healthier lifestyle. As part of your program, every day you will get articles, tips and advice from Canyon Ranch experts, plus delicious recipes created by our chefs and exclusive videos to give you more information and inspiration to reach and sustain your weight goal.

Easy-to-Use Tools and Trackers

You can start a video session with the click of a button, read articles specially chosen for you by your coaches and track your weight, calories, exercise and sleep every day on your private dashboard. (Also syncs with Jawbone UP® band or Fitbit®.)

Change That Lasts a Lifetime

Our program is designed to deliver results that last a lifetime. Our experts will not only set you up for success, they will stay with you month after month to keep you on track. Your plan will be reviewed and updated by your coaches based on the results you’re seeing and feeling.

About our program

Over the course of this 6-month program, you will receive:

1 Orientation Session

A member of our team will personally welcome you to the program, walk you through the dashboard and features, conduct a test video session and schedule your first coaching sessions.

3 Intake Video Coaching Sessions (Month 1)

In order to get to know you and develop a plan that meets your needs, you will have three 45-minute intake sessions with your coaches during your first month. You’ll meet with one of our nutrition coaches and one of our fitness coaches, plus your life coach who will work with you throughout the entire program.

3 Core Video Coaching Sessions per Month (Months 2-6)

Coaches in these disciplines will follow up with you in 20-minute sessions to check in on your progress and continue to modify your goals—and the strategies to help you meet them.

6 Floater Video Coaching Sessions per Program

Helpful for when you feel you need just a bit more support, you may schedule these 20-minute floater sessions to speak with our fitness, nutrition or life coaches whenever you’d like.*

*See Terms and Conditions for full details.
